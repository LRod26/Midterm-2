Denzel Washington

My Favorite Movies!

Man On Fire (2004)

Rank #1

Man On Fire is probably my favorite Denzel Washington movie of all time. It takes place in Mexico. It's about an officer by the name of John W. Creasy (played by Denzel Washington) who goes to all ends to save a kidnapped girl. This movie highlights the big problem (and common occurance) that was going on in Mexico City, which was the kidnapping of children. My favorite quote from the movie is "Forgiveness is between them and God. It is my job to arrange the meeting."

Training Day (2001)

Rank #2

Training Day is one of the earlier movies that I saw of Denzel Washington. In this movie, Denzel plays Alonzo Harris who happens to be a crooked officer from the Los Angeles Police Department. I like this movie because it opens the curtain on the behind the scenes of things that occur with the police. I think we sometimes forget that they are human as well who can fall into temptation.

Flight (2012)

Rank #3

The movie Flight is about Whip Whitaker (Played by Denzel Washington) who is an excellent airplane pilot, but as we learn later in the movie, he has a bit of problem. The reason why I love this movie so much is because I have the oportunity to fly often (to Mexico) and just to see the realistic wreckage of a flight dropping sends chills down my spine. I can't explain it, I would never want to be in that situation, but it's one of those things where you don't want to watch and can't stop looking at the same time.
